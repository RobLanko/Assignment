Hobbies

My favorite hobby is hiking/camping. Just being in the outdoors is fun, and I appreciate nature alot more because of it. When I say the outdoors, I mean everything from cooking all the way to foraging small fruits like raspberries and strawberries.

My second favorite Hobby would have to be music. I love listening to all types of music, and singing along with it as well! Not long ago, I started learning how to create beats on my Macbook!

Last but not least, video games take the 3rd place. I've played video games my whole life, starting at a young age with an atari, all the way up to the latest consoles today. Video games have helpedme create and bond relationships easier than if I was out in public.
